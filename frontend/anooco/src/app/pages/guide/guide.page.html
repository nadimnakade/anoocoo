User Guide

Welcome to Anooco

Your intelligent road companion. Stay safe, informed, and connected.

Dashboard & Map

The Map: Shows your current location and nearby alerts (potholes, accidents, police, etc.).

View Modes: Tap the "Map/List" button in the bottom sheet to toggle between:

  • Split: Map on top, alerts list below.
  • Map Only: Full screen map.
  • List Only: Full screen list of alerts.

Expired Alerts: Use the "Eye" icon in the top menu to show/hide expired or old alerts.

 Reporting Incidents

Voice Reporting: Tap the microphone button or say "Hey Anooco" (if Hands-free is on). Then say:

  • "Report accident"
  • "Report pothole"
  • "Report traffic"
  • "Report police"

Manual Reporting: Tap the "Manual" button to select an incident type from the grid.

Hands-free Mode: Tap "Auto On/Off" to enable background listening for "Hey Anooco". Ideal for driving.

 Tools & Features

Dashcam: Access via the Tools menu. Connects to compatible WiFi dashcams to view and download footage.

Road Sign OCR: Use your camera to scan road signs. The app detects speed limits and warns you if you exceed them.

Pothole Detection: The app uses your phone's accelerometer to automatically detect severe bumps and report potential potholes.

 Settings & Customization

Profile: Update your username and view your Trust Score.

Mute Settings: Configure a mute radius (e.g., around your home) or specific streets where you don't want audio alerts.

Calibration: Use the Tools menu to calibrate the pothole detection sensitivity for your specific vehicle/mount.
